How to read this

What "shared position" means.

A shared position is a stance the coalition has reached consensus on, meaning every member organization either supports it or stands aside without blocking. Shared positions reflect what the coalition can agree to as a group at a given moment.

Listing as a member of the coalition does not mean every member endorses every shared position, and the coalition does not bind members on issues outside the shared position itself. When member organizations want to act in their own names, they do.
